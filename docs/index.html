Preserving Spatial Cues in Music Source Separation

Here we show the results achieved by SA-HTDemucs. We extend Hybrid Transformer Demucs with lightweight per-source SpatialCueModules that explicitly supervise Interaural Level Differences (ILD), preventing the phantom-centre collapse that standard mono-trained separators produce. Each separated stem retains a perceptually meaningful stereo position without sacrificing separation quality.
